About Everest

EVEREST TECH is a company registered in Singapore, specializing in semiconductor process technology development. We offer semiconductor equipment performance testing services. Our team has extensive experience in the R&D of advanced semiconductor process technologies, including gallium nitride and silicon photonics. We are well-versed in the selection, testing, installation, operation, and maintenance of key semiconductor equipment (e.g. ASML lithography machines, Nikon lithography machines, AMAT, TEL, KLA, ASM, etc.).

Our Team

Our core team comprises more than 30 professional technical experts, who have been dedicated to their respective fields for over twenty years. Team members have extensive working experience in renowned domestic and foreign enterprises and research institutions, such as ASML, the Institute of Microelectronics (IME) of Singapore, SSMC, and Hua Hong Grace Semiconductor Manufacturing Co., Ltd. We have abundant technical reserves and rich practical experience.

More About Us

EVEREST TECH has always been committed to providing enterprises and research institutes with semiconductor-related technical services of international and advanced standards. We aim to expand into more international markets, as well as to offer an even broader range of professional services.
